Features

Everything Your Business Needs

Our system is designed to save you time. Forget manual data entry into accounting systems.

Easy Importing

Upload invoices in PDF, JPG, PNG, WEBP format. System supports bulk upload up to 10 files at once. Duplicate invoices are automatically detected.

Automated Data Extraction

Advanced technology automatically extracts all data from invoices: amounts, dates, companies, item lists and VAT details.

Automatic Company Recognition

System automatically identifies and links buyer and seller with your company database. Edit and confirm data before export.

Export to Accounting Systems

Export data in JSON, XML, CSV, TXT format. Create custom export templates or send data directly via API calls based on your accounting system requirements.

Filtering and Grouping

Filter invoices by companies, dates, statuses. Export by company — system automatically groups invoices by buyer or seller.

AI Agent Integration

Connect with AI Agents via Model Context Protocol (MCP). Let your favorite AI assistants (Cursor, Claude) read, analyze and manage your invoices directly.

Highest Security

Self-hosted solution where all data stays on your machine. Data encryption, regular backups and local control ensure the security of your financial data.
